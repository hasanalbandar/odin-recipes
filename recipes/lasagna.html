This recipe is for the dish as old as time, classic Italian style Lasagna.

Lasagna originates from the Italian Peninsula and has been a classic dish that can be enjoyed for both lunch and dinner. It is a common dish enjoyed here in Australia too so let's learn how to make this beauty!

We will now be providing a list of ingridients and steps to bring this magical dish to life!

Ingridients

  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Tomato Paste
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Oregano
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • 1kg minced beef

Now that we have procured our ingridients let's look at the steps to cooking this up.

Steps

  1. Place the layers of sheets in boiling water for 15 min
  2. Cook the beef until brown for 10 min
  3. Layer the tomatoes, beef and cheese on the sheets
  4. Place in oven for 90 minutes at 360 degrees

And there you have it, a classic Italian lasagna ready to enjoy!
